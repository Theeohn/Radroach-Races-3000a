### Description

A version of the internet famous "Horse Race Tests" game now for your pipboy. Start the game up, three seconds to place your bets and they're off! Using logic similar to the DVD bouncing logo to race to the mutfruit at the end, who will win? Five seperate tracks, five shapes for your roaches. Endless hours of fun watching, betting, and forming your own headcanons!

### Controls

- Press the left knob to begin the race.
- Press the left knob again to start another!
- Press and of the bottom three buttons to exit the game, or power button to reset the device.
